Chocolate Chess Pie

This chocolate chess pie is so easy to make for a rich, gooey, and absolutely delicious chocolate dessert — great for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (9-inch) unbaked pie crust

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix sugar and cocoa together in a large bowl. Beat eggs in a medium bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy; stir into cocoa mixture. Beat in evaporated milk, butter, and vanilla. Pour mixture into unbaked pie crust.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until set, about 45 minutes. Let cool before slicing.
