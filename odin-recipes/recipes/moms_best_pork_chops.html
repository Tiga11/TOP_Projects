Mom's Best Pork Chops

Try this recipe for thinly sliced pork chops with a crunchy breaded coating for tender and juicy chops that are sure to be everyone's favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • 3 (4 ounce) packets saltine crackers, crushed
  • 6 thin cut pork chops
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. Whisk eggs, garlic powder, and onion powder together in a shallow bowl. Place cracker crumbs into a second shallow bowl.
  4. Dip pork chops into egg mixture, then press in cracker crumbs to coat.
  5. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add breaded chops and fry until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Transfer to a baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center, about 45 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
